# -E-COMMERCE-SALES-ANALYSIS
This project Analyze an online retail company’s sales data to answer key business questions and provide actionable insights using pandas and numpy. The goal is to:

*Clean messy transactional data
*Handle duplicates and missing values
*Perform exploratory data analysis (EDA)
*Answer key business questions
*Generate actionable insights

Data Source: The dataset used in this project was obtained from: Kaggle
Dataset Name: Online Retail Link: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/vijayuv/onlineretail?select=OnlineRetail.csv

Business Questions

A. Basic Overview
-What is the total revenue generated?
-How many orders and unique customers are there?
-What is the total quantity of items sold?
-What is the average order value?
-How many unique products are sold?
B. Customer Analysis
-Who are the top 10 customers by total revenue?
-Which customers buy most frequently (number of orders)
-How many customers made only one purchase?
-What is the distribution of customer revenue (e.g., low, medium, high)?
-Who are the customers with highest returns or cancellations?
C. Product Analysis
-Which products generate the most revenue?
-What are the top 10 products by quantity sold?
-Are there products with negative quantities (returns)?
-Which products are rarely sold or low demand items?
-Which products are frequently returned?
D. Country & Region Analysis
-Which countries generate the highest revenue?
-How many orders come from each country?
-What is the average order value per country?
E. Time-Based Analysis
-How do monthly sales trend over time?
-Which day of the week has the highest sales?

-Are there seasonal spikes in revenue (holidays, weekends)?

-How does revenue vary by hour of the day?
